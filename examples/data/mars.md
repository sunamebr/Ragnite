# Mars

Mars is the fourth planet from the Sun and the second-smallest planet in the
solar system. Its surface is covered in iron oxide dust, which gives the planet
its characteristic red appearance.

## Moons

Mars has two small, irregularly shaped moons: Phobos and Deimos. Both are
thought to be captured asteroids.

## Geology

Olympus Mons on Mars is the tallest volcano in the solar system, rising about
22 km above the surrounding plains. Valles Marineris is a canyon system that
stretches over 4,000 km along the equator.

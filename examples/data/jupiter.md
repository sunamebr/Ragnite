# Jupiter

Jupiter is the largest planet in the solar system — more than twice as massive
as all the other planets combined.

## The Great Red Spot

The Great Red Spot is a persistent anticyclonic storm larger than Earth that
has been observed for centuries.

## Moons

Jupiter has dozens of moons. The four largest — Io, Europa, Ganymede and
Callisto — were discovered by Galileo in 1610. Europa's subsurface ocean makes
it a prime candidate in the search for life.
